About me

Borned creative, yet nonartistic, I was never able to illustrate my creativeness through drawings and paintings. One day, out of boredom, I created my first program during my sophomore year of highschool in 2011, making a simple guessing game that my friends and I played and laughed together. The game didn't last long -- probably only a couple hours -- but ever since that day, I realized that there was a way to portray my creativeness, not through arts, but via programming. Thus, upon graduating high school, I began my journey as a software engineer in San Jose State University.

In my spare time, I do a variety of activities. I enjoy playing video games; as a semi-competitive gamer, I've been placed in top rankings in couple of local tournaments and official ladder in several different games. I also enjoy watching TV shows, especially ones with genre relating to crime, mystery and action. I occasionally play badminton, tennis, and ping pong -- is it weird that those are all racket sports...?
