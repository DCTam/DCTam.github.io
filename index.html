About me

Because I have never been artistic in the conventional sense, I have always struggled to express my creativity through drawings or paintings. But one day I created my first program in my sophomore year of high school. Out of pure boredom and curiosity, I developed a simple guessing game just for my friends and me to play with and laugh over. The game didn't last long; it ended in less than a couple hours. However, since then, I realized that there was a way to portray my creativity, not through arts as I had previously understood, but through programming. Thus, upon graduating high school, I began my journey as a software engineer at San Jose State University.

Outside of programming, I try to spread out my interests. One of my favorite activities is playing video games. As a self-proclaimed competitive gamer, I have frequently ranked at the top of several official leaderboards and placed in many local tournaments. I also enjoy watching television related to crime, mystery, and action. As my attempt to balance things out with a bit of physical activity, I also enjoy playing badminton, tennis, and ping pong.
